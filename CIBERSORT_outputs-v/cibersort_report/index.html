  • P-value indicates the statistical significance of the CIBERSORT results, helping to determine whether the observed cell type proportions are likely to be reliable or due to random chance.

  • Root Mean Square Error (RMSE) provides a measure of the average deviation of the predicted cell type proportions from the actual values, with lower values indicating more accurate predictions.
  • The sum of fractions should equal one, reflecting the total contribution of all cell types in the sample, ensuring that the results are biologically interpretable and consistent.
